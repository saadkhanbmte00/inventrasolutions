Senior engineers who own the hard parts

INVENTRA is a focused design & automation studio. We combine hardware, firmware and controls expertise to turn ambitious ideas into dependable, production-grade systems.

Our story

Born on the factory floor, built for the future

INVENTRA started with a simple frustration: too many automation projects stall between disciplines — the hardware team waits on firmware, firmware waits on controls, and the integrator blames everyone. We built a team that closes those gaps.

Today we partner with manufacturers, product companies and startups to design, build and deploy systems across the full stack — from SolidWorks mechanical design and ESP32/STM32 firmware to B&R and Siemens PLC/HMI lines and cloud-connected IoT.

We stay small on purpose. Every project gets senior attention, honest advice, and an engineer who answers the phone long after go-live.

What we stand for

Principles we engineer by

Reliability first

If it can't run unattended for years, it isn't done. We design for failure modes, not just the happy path.

Pragmatic speed

We ship working prototypes early and iterate with real feedback. Momentum beats perfection on a whiteboard.

Radical clarity

Plain-language updates, honest trade-offs and no jargon walls. You always understand what you're paying for.

Whole-system thinking

We optimise the system, not the silo — hardware, firmware, controls and UX considered together.

Documentation as a deliverable

CAD, source, runbooks and handover docs come standard. Your team is never locked in.

Long-term partnership

We measure success in v2, v3 and the years of uptime after — not in a single invoice.
