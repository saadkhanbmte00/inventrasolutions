Engineering services, end to end

Six disciplines under one roof — so your hardware, firmware, controls and connectivity actually work together. Pick a single service or hand us the whole problem.

Industrial Automation & PLC/HMI

Design, programming and commissioning of automated lines and machines. We build maintainable PLC logic and operator-friendly HMIs that production teams actually like using.

  • B&R, Siemens & Codesys PLC programming
  • HMI / panel design & UX
  • Retrofits, line integration & commissioning

Embedded Systems & Firmware

From prototype to shippable firmware. We build reliable, low-power embedded products on ESP32, STM32 and Arduino — with the production discipline most prototypes skip.

  • Bare-metal & RTOS firmware (C/C++/Rust)
  • Sensors, motor control & power management
  • Diagnostics, calibration & field reliability

IoT & Connectivity

Connect machines and products to the cloud — securely. We build the gateways, protocols and dashboards that turn raw signals into operational insight.

  • MQTT, OPC UA & edge gateways
  • Real-time dashboards (Grafana / custom)
  • Time-series storage & alerting

Control Systems & SCADA

Closed-loop control, motion and plant-wide supervision. We design control strategies that stay stable under real-world disturbances — and give operators full visibility.

  • PID / model-based control & tuning
  • Motion & multi-axis drives
  • SCADA, alarms & historian

Mechanical Design & Prototyping

Turn a concept into a manufacturable product. SolidWorks 3D CAD, enclosure and rapid prototyping with design-for-manufacture baked in from day one.

  • SolidWorks 3D CAD & mechanical design
  • Enclosure / CAD & 3D-printed prototypes
  • DFM, BOM optimisation & pilot runs

Consulting & Training

Senior engineering judgement when you need it. Architecture reviews, technical due diligence, audits and hands-on training to level up your in-house team.

  • System architecture & technology selection
  • Code / design audits & due diligence
  • Workshops & team upskilling
